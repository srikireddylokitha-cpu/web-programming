Emergency Helpline Numbers in India

National Emergency Number
Description: Single emergency helpline number for police, fire, and medical emergencies across India.

Helpline Number: 112
Email: support@112.gov.in

Police
Description: For reporting crimes, theft, accidents, or law and order issues.

Helpline Number: 100
Email: police@nic.in

Ambulance / Medical Emergency
Description: Immediate medical assistance and ambulance services.

Helpline Number: 108
Email: ambulance@health.in

Fire Brigade
Description: For fire accidents and rescue operations.

Helpline Number: 101
Email: fire@nic.in

Women Helpline
Description: Support and protection for women facing harassment or violence.

Helpline Number: 181
Email: womenhelpline@nic.in

Child Helpline
Description: Assistance for children in distress, abuse, or exploitation.

Helpline Number: 1098
Email: childline@nic.in

Disaster Management
Description: Help during natural disasters such as floods, earthquakes, and cyclones.

Helpline Number: 1078
Email: disaster@ndma.gov.in

Senior Citizen Helpline
Description: Assistance and protection for senior citizens.

Helpline Number: 14567
Email: seniorcare@nic.in

Anti-Ragging Helpline

Helpline Number: 1800-180-5522

Road Accident Emergency Service
Description: Emergency response for road accidents and highway assistance.

Helpline Number: 1033
